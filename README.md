# Vrinda Store Data Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Vrinda Store's sales performance for the year 2022 and identify customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and growth opportunities. The insights generated from this analysis can help the business improve sales and make data-driven decisions for 2023.

---

## Dataset
- Vrinda Store Sales Data (2022)

---

## Business Questions (KPIs)

1. Compare Sales and Orders using a single chart.
2. Which month recorded the highest Sales and Orders?
3. Who purchased more products: Men or Women?
4. What were the different Order Status categories in 2022?
5. Which Top 10 States contributed the most to Sales?
6. What is the relationship between Age Group and Gender based on Orders?
7. Which Sales Channel contributed the most revenue?
8. Which Product Category generated the highest sales?

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation

- Checked and handled missing values.
- Removed inconsistencies and data anomalies.
- Standardized data types and formats.
- Verified data accuracy and quality.
- Created Pivot Tables for KPI analysis.
- Built an interactive dashboard using charts and slicers.

---

## Dashboard Features

- Sales vs Orders Trend Analysis
- Sales by Gender
- Order Status Distribution
- Top Performing States
- Age Group vs Gender Analysis
- Channel-wise Sales Contribution
- Interactive Filters (Slicers)
  
- ## Key Insights

- Women customers contributed approximately **65%** of total purchases.
- **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** were the top-performing states.
- The **Adult Age Group (30–49 years)** generated the highest number of orders (~50%).
- Most orders were placed through **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**.
- The majority of orders were successfully delivered, indicating strong operational performance.

-## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Dashboard Design
- Data Cleaning & Analysis


## Final Conclusion

Based on the analysis, women customers aged 30–49 years from Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh represent the most valuable customer segment. By targeting this audience through major online marketplaces such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra with personalized offers and promotions, Vrinda Store can significantly improve sales and customer retention.
